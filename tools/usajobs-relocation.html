Application checklist

  • Create USAJOBS account + build resume in builder format
  • Upload SF-50 / DD-214 if prior federal or military service
  • Filter Location: Overseas and series matching your skills (0343, 0340, 2210, 1102, etc.)
  • Apply to multiple series — admin, IT, logistics, program analyst
  • In questionnaire: willing to relocate internationally — yes
  • Parallel track: NGO deal pack (often faster than federal hiring)

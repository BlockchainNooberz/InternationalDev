Research & transparency dashboard — not an activism platform

This module aggregates publicly published governance indicators (Freedom House, Reporters Without Borders, Transparency International, World Justice Project) for academic, NGO, and policy research. It does not collect citizen dissent, organize opposition, or target political change campaigns. Publishing dissent inside Russia carries severe legal risk — this tool is for external researchers only.
